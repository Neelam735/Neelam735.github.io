## About

Chess is a classic two-player strategy board game featuring:
- Full chess rules including castling, en passant and pawn promotion
- Beautiful chess board with clear piece distinction
- Move hints with legal move highlighting
- Move history in algebraic notation
- Captured pieces tracking
- Check and checkmate detection
- No internet required - play offline anytime
